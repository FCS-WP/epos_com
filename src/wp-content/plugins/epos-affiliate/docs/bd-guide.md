## 1. Getting Started

### First Login

You will receive a **welcome email** when your account is created. The email contains:
- Your **username**
- Your **temporary password**
- The **reseller** you belong to
- A link to the **login page**

**Steps:**
1. Open the login link from your email: `/my/login/`
2. Enter your username and password
3. You will be redirected to your **BD Dashboard**

> **Important:** Change your password after your first login. Go to **Profile → Change Password**.

### Login URL

```
https://www.epos.com/my/login/
```

After logging in, you are automatically redirected to your dashboard at `/my/dashboard/bd/`.

## 3. Your QR Code

Your QR code is your main sales tool. When a customer scans it, they are taken directly to the product checkout page and the sale is attributed to you.

### How it works

```
Customer scans your QR code
  → They see the EPOS product page with product pre-added to cart
  → They complete checkout and pay normally
  → The order is automatically linked to you
  → A commission is recorded for you
```

The customer sees a **normal checkout** — they don't see your name, tracking code, or any referral info.

### Viewing your QR Code

- On your **Dashboard**, the QR code is displayed in the top card
- Go to **QR Code** page from the sidebar for the full-size view

### Actions

| Action | What it does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Copy Link** | Copy your QR URL to clipboard — paste it into WhatsApp, Telegram, or any messaging app |
| **Download PNG** | Save the QR code as a high-quality image (600×600 pixels) — print it, add to brochures, name cards |
| **Share** | Use your phone's native share feature to send via any app |

### Your QR URL

```
https://www.epos.com/my/qr/[YOUR_TOKEN]
```

> **Tip:** The URL works the same as scanning the QR image. You can share the link directly via messaging apps when you can't show the QR code in person.

### Best Practices

- Print your QR code and attach it to your name card or brochure
- Share the QR link via WhatsApp/Telegram to remote customers
- The QR code never expires (as long as your account is active)

### Profile Photo

Click the camera icon on your avatar to upload a new photo.

### Change Password

1. Scroll down to the **Change Password** section
2. Enter your **current password**
3. Enter your **new password** (minimum 8 characters)
4. Enter the new password again to **confirm**
5. Click **"Change Password"**

**Password requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- Must be different from your current password

## 6. FAQ

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A: On the login page at `/my/login/`, click "Forgot Password?" to reset via email.

**Q: Can other BDs see my orders or performance?**
A: No. You can only see your own data. Other BDs and resellers cannot see your orders or commissions.

**Q: Can I see how much commission I've earned?**
A: Your commissions are tracked by the admin team. Contact your reseller manager or admin for commission details.

**Q: My QR code is not working. What should I do?**
A: Make sure your account is active. If you see a login error about your account being disabled, contact your reseller manager or admin.

**Q: Can I have more than one QR code?**
A: No. Each BD has one unique QR code linked to their tracking code. All orders from your QR code are attributed to you.

**Q: What if a customer doesn't scan the QR but uses the link instead?**
A: The link works exactly the same as scanning the QR code. The order will be attributed to you either way.

**Q: How do I get paid?**
A: Commissions are reviewed and approved by the admin, then paid to your bank account. Make sure your bank details on the Profile page are correct and up to date.

**Q: I can't log in and see "account disabled".**
A: Your account has been deactivated by your reseller manager or admin. Contact them to reactivate your account.

**Q: Can I change my tracking code or QR code?**
A: No. Your tracking code and QR code are permanent and cannot be changed. They are linked to your sales history.
